Welcome to Piggy Planner! 🐷

Here is how your cozy finance journal works. Let's get your budget started!

1. The Concept

Think of this as your physical paper planner. Everything saves automatically so you can safely log and allocate your funds offline or in the cloud.

2. Add Income

Set your starting balance and list paychecks or gigs under Income. Click any cell to type. This defines your monthly budget pool!

3. Log Obligations

Add monthly bills and debt obligations in the middle column. Type due dates and budgets, then check them off as they are paid off!

4. Track & Allocate

Distribute the rest to Expenses. Set your asset allocation percentages to draw the hand-drawn wheel in the right column!
